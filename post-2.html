The Exam That Never Asked What Happiness Is

But... now that I think about it... Happiness is... living snugly with my family and friends...

That means "peace."

But to be such peaceful, one must be strong. And strong power is visible power: money. So, I must earn money.

Happiness is sweet, but peace is just old-fashioned and worn out. The effort required for peace is uncomfortable, painful, and even agonizing.

So today, to earn money, instead of a warm, boring tea that soothes the body, I drink an Iced Americano and an energy drink to snap awake from my lack of sleep.
