DRAFT BOARD

Apple.

2026-03-12

Steve Jobs probably wanted Apple to collapse without him.
Tim Cook is the gatekeeper he appointed —
a butler tasked with witnessing the final days of a beautiful castle called Apple.
To carry on his will, the iPhone stopped innovating.
To preserve Steve Jobs' most perfect aesthetic masterpiece,
change itself was brought to a halt.
Jobs wanted that.
He had saved Apple — the very company that once cast him out.
So perhaps he wished for it to vanish the moment he did.

This is just my imagination.

🙂
