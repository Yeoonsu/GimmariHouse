Regarding the Publication of 'How to Fail in South Korea'

2026-02-07

On the Journey of "How to Be Brave"

I am currently working on a heartwarming essay about my recovery and the people who gave me the courage to move forward. While the title is “How to Fail in South Korea,” its true heart lies in “How to Be Brave.”

I have decided not to release this current essay to the world. I found myself constantly burdened by the thought that using the precious goodwill extended to me as mere material for my work might betray the trust I owe as an artist. While I know that creating a new world requires a long and patient journey, I feel a sense of shame for letting my impatience get ahead of the natural pace of true craft.

Instead, I plan to make my work with a novel. Creating an entire world is a long and arduous journey, and it will take time. I feel a sense of shame for letting my impatience get ahead of the natural pace of true craft.

— Mary Kim, St. Agnes.
