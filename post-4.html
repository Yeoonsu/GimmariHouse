Writing for Alien

2026-02-18

외계인을 위한 글쓰기

"너 글을 잘 쓰네."라는 칭찬은 마치 "밥을 복스럽게 잘 먹네."와 같이 느껴졌다. 밥을 유난히 남들보다 맛있게 먹는다는 사실은 어릴 때부터 익히 들어와서 잘 알고 있었지만, 사회에서 사람들과 함께 살아야 하는 나는 그 재능을 쓰기 위해서 반드시 먹방 유투버로서 상업적으로 큰 성공을 거두어야만 한다. 그렇게 재능이 비로소 눈에 보여야만 인정 받을 수 있는 것이다.

25년 3월 스레드에 올린 게시물은 자고 일어나니 34만명의 유저에게 노출되었고, 1만 개의 좋아요를 받았다. 놀라운 일은 아니었다. 이미 여러번 겪어왔기에. 또 다시 이어지는 신상 추측과 진실에 대한 공방이 피곤해서 계정을 삭제했을 뿐이다.

To the Alien,

​Many told me that because I am so ordinary, I would never be able to focus on just one thing. They said I should "do my best" at the things humans do. ​But I didn't go to university to win a Nobel Prize. I wandered simply to find something worth writing about.

No one believes that I typed for 18 hours without a break. When the next day comes and I feel my passion and plans are untouchable and out of reach, I am left to endure an excruciating and horrific phantom pain. It leaves me in a state of horrific despair.

But I know I don't need long explanations for you, alien, to believe in my sincerity.

​I write in English to find empathy. Surely, a civilized extraterrestrial capable of communication would use the electromagnetic frequency of this global tongue?

A few have already perceived the reality—that my nature is not human—and they have left me. Are you also out there, somewhere distant, alone?

​Van Gogh believed Gauguin was an alien. He obsessed over him until Gauguin, burdened by that weight, fled. Then Van Gogh cut off his own ear. To avoid a fate like his, I must diligently search for others of my kind.

​How can I, who must live among humans, ever reveal that I am merely an alien posing as one? I look like them, so they will doubt. They will demand proof.

​That is why my writing must feel like static—a secret, difficult noise that the uninterested will simply bypass.

An alien would tell me it's okay to stop 'coding' and just 'write'. In this vast universe, you must exist, so I must keep trying. ​ I trust the algorithm to bring this to you.

🛸 Mary Kim. Draft.
