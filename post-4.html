Can I Become a Butterfly?

2026-02-15

Who would ever read this site? I deliberately did not set up a view counter when I built this website. This website is highly inaccessible.

I live in a Korean community, yet the language here is English. It is uncomfortable to read. And yet, am I waiting for that one person who will read it regardless?

I struggle with Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder. While I criticize a society that refuses to believe in invisible forces, I, in turn, cannot trust the visible.

No matter how intelligent I am, I cannot control others or the world. Because I fear variables I cannot control, I over-control myself instead.

My emotional swings are severe, and I often make mistakes. I know that my problems will repeat themselves.

I love people so much that I expect too much of them on my own terms, only to be disappointed.

I think I am smart.

And I am always blaming myself.

People do not like looking into deep, profound emotions. I don’t really know where I should go.
